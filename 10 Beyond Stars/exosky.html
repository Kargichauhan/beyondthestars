Our featured exoplanets list

HD 222237 b

HD 222237 b is a gas giant exoplanet that orbits a K-type star. Its mass is 5.19 Jupiters, it takes 40.8 years to complete one orbit of its star, and is 10.8 AU from its star. Its discovery was announced in 2024.

TOI-1798.01

TOI-1798.01 is a Neptune-like exoplanet that orbits a K-type star. Its mass is 6.5 Earths, it takes 8 days to complete one orbit of its star, and is 0.074 AU from its star. Its discovery was announced in 2024.

Kepler-85 e

Kepler-85 e is a super Earth exoplanet that orbits a G-type star. Its mass is 0.6 Earths, it takes 25.2 days to complete one orbit of its star, and is 0.163 AU from its star. Its discovery was announced in 2014.

KOI-55 b

Kepler-1558 b

Kepler-1558 b is a terrestrial exoplanet that orbits a K-type star. Its mass is 0.244 Earths, it takes 3.5 days to complete one orbit of its star, and is 0.0419 AU from its star. Its discovery was announced in 2016.

KMT-2019-BLG-0297L b

KMT-2019-BLG-0297L b is a gas giant exoplanet that orbits an unknown-type star. Its mass is 19 Jupiters, it takes 2.1 years to complete one orbit of its star, and is 0.96 AU from its star. Its discovery was announced in 2023.
