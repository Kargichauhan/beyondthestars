Kepler-85 e

Super Earth

Kepler-85 e is a super Earth exoplanet that orbits a G-type star. Its mass is 0.6 Earths, it takes 25.2 days to complete one orbit of its star, and is 0.163 AU from its star. Its discovery was announced in 2014.
